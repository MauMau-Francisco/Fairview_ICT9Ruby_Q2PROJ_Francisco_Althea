Ginataang Bilo-Bilo

Ingredients needed

- Two coconut milk cans

- One can of coconut cream

- Condensed milk, half a cup

- Two pieces of pandan leaves

- Peel and chop one large sweet potato into bite-sized pieces

- jack fruit in heavy syrup, half a jar (1 cup)

- One cup of bottled macapuno

- Two cups of fried tiny sago (tapioca pearls)

- Four cups of saba, peeled and slived to a minimum thickness of 1/2 inch a few drops of extract from ube.

Procedure:

- Bring a pot of coconut milk, cream, condensed milk, and pandan leaves to a boil.

- Add sweet potatoes and cook for 7 minutes on high heat.

- Add the rice balls, sliced jack fruit, and its syrup after adjusting the heat to medium.

- Put the tapioca pearl in and Stir in the macapuno.

- Put bananas in there. Simmer for two minutes over low heat, stirring occasionally, warm up and serve.
