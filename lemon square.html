Lemon Square

Ingredients needed

- 6 pieces of Lemon Square Cheese CakeÂ®

- Condensed Milk, 150 ml

- Graham Crackers (or crushed Grahams): 6 pieces

- One-third cup of lemon juice

- A tablespoon of butter

Procedure:

FOR THE GRAHAM CRUST
- Chop the graham crackers.

- Add butter to crumbled Grahams.

FOR THE LEMON FILLING
- Add half a cup of lemon juice and 150 milliliters of condensed milk.

- Cover Grahams with 1/4 cup of the condensed milk and lemon juice mixture.

- Crush the lemon square with a fork and stir in 1/4 cup lemon juice.

- Cover the milk mixture with smashed lemon squares.

- Spread the remaining milk-lemon mixture evenly over the top.

- Let cool for roughly three hours.

- Before serving, garnish with sliced lemon (optional).
