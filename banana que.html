Banana Que

Ingredients needed

- 15–18 saba bananas in a bunch (or 3–4 regular bananas or 2 plantains)

- 3 heaping tablespoons of brown sugar (palm sugar also works nicely)

- Frying oil for frying

- Sesame seeds, if desired

Procedure:

- Heat oil in a wok (deep fryer, deep cast iron pan, etc.) until it is between 1½ and 2 inches high. (I set my oil temperature to 360°F for regular bananas and 350°F for thicker, larger ones.)

- Once heated, sauté the bananas over medium heat for one to two minutes (two minutes if you're using larger bananas than Thai ones). Gently drop the bananas one at a time.

- To ensure that the bananas cook evenly, turn them a few times and then add the brown sugar

- To get the sugar to caramelize in the oil, turn the bananas ONE time and maintain the heat on medium and DO NOT STIR.

- As soon as the sugar begins to caramelize, begin rearranging the bananas until all of them have been completely covered in caramel.

- Cook gently for three to five minutes
