Kare-Kare

Ingredients needed

- 1/4 kg beef

- 1/4 kg tripe

- 1 cup of peanut butter

- 1/4 cup grounded toasted rice

- 1/2 cup cooked Bagoong Alamang

- 1 large onion, diced

- 3 cloves of garlic, minced

- 1 tablespoon atsuete seeds

- 2 pieces eggplant, sliced 1 inch thick

- 1 bundle Bok Choy (Pechay)

- 1 bundle of String Beans

- G1 bundle of okra

- 3 tbsp. oil

- 5 cups of water

- Salt to taste

Procedure:

- The tripe and beef should be cooked after an hour of boiling in water.

- After straining, preserve the stock.

- In a pan, heat the oil and atsuete seeds. The seeds were taken out.

- Saut√© the onions and garlic until they turn golden.

- Stir in peanut butter, toasted rice, and stock. Add the tripe and meat.

- After bringing to a boil, simmer for fifteen minutes and season with salt.

- Add the bok choy, okra, string beans, and eggplant (if available only).

- Give the veggies five minutes to cook.

- Avoid overcooking the veggies.

- Serve with bagoong and rice.
