Halo-Halo

Ingredients needed

- One 20-oz can of syrup-soaked jackfruit

- Two-thirds cup of full-fat, unsweetened coconut milk in a can

- Three teaspoons of milk that has been evaporated

- Condensed milk, sweetened, three teaspoons

- Ube or coconut ice cream, four scoops

- Minatamis na saging and tapioca

- Macapuno, or coconut sport strings, half a cup

- Half a cup ube halaya

- 1 1/2 cups of coco nata

- Four glasses of ice cream, plus extra

Procedure:

- After taking the jackfruit out of a 20-ounce can, discard the syrup. Cut the jackfruit roughly into small pieces. Put the can of jackfruit back in it.

- In a small bowl or liquid measuring cup, combine 2/3 cup coconut milk, 3 tablespoons evaporated milk, and 3 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk. Stir to mix.

- To make one serving, arrange the ingredients in a 16-ounce glass in the following order: 1 scoop of coconut or ube ice cream, 2 tablespoons of macapuno, 2 tablespoons of ube halaya, 1/4 cup of jackfruit and syrup, 1/3 cup of coco de nata, and 1/3 cup of minatamis na saging. Drizzle 3 tablespoons of the milk mixture over 1 cup of shaved ice. If necessary, fill the glass with more shaved ice.

- Serve and Enjoy!.
